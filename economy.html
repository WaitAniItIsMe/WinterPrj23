murshidabad:
economy

Agriculture: Agriculture plays a crucial role in Murshidabad's economy due to its fertile Gangetic. The rich alluvial soil and proximity to the Bhagirathi River contribute to the agricultural productivity of the region.
Handloom Industry: Murshidabad has a strong tradition of handloom weaving, particularly silk weaving. The city is renowned for Murshidabad silk, which is characterized by its fine texture and intricate designs.
Toursim: Murshidabad's historical and cultural heritage attracts tourists, contributing to the local economy. Landmarks like Hazarduari Palace, Katra Masjid, and other historical sites draw visitors interested in the city's rich history.
